## Daymark: AI-powered data discovery for GTM and product teams

[Daymark](https://www.usedaymark.io/) helps non-technical teams get fast, reliable answers from their business data without writing SQL, waiting on analysts, or stitching together spreadsheets. You ask a question in plain English, and Daymark generates the query logic, pulls from the right sources, and surfaces the answer.

### The problem it solves

Most teams are drowning in data but starving for answers.

Metrics live across spreadsheets, BI tools, databases, and Slack threads.
Simple questions sit in analyst queues for days.
Different teams use different definitions for the same KPI.
Reports shared last month are already stale.

Daymark collapses all of this into a single workspace where answers take minutes, not days.

### What Daymark does

Daymark connects your data sources and lets anyone on your team query them in plain English.

You ask a question like "show me sessions and revenue by channel for the last 7 days."
Daymark generates the query logic, pulls from the right sources, and returns a chart.
Cross-source questions work too — correlate HubSpot pipeline data with Google Ads spend in a single query.

Everyone works from the same live data, with shared metric definitions and shareable dashboards that don't go stale.

### Key features

* Natural language querying — no SQL required, though SQL users can validate and refine outputs
* Multi-source integration across PostgreSQL, Google Sheets, HubSpot, Google Analytics, Google Ads, Meta Ads, Google Search Console, and Shopify
* Cross-source analysis to answer questions that span multiple tools in one query
* AI-powered insights that surface trends without training on your data or touching production systems
* Shareable dashboards and reports so teams stay aligned on a single source of truth
* Read-only, least-privilege access with no data used for model training

### How it works

Connect your first data source in minutes — Google Sheets, PostgreSQL, or a CSV upload.
Ask a question in plain English.
Get a query, chart, and answer automatically.
Share it with your team or save it as a live dashboard.

### Who it is for

Daymark is built for GTM and product teams — marketing, sales, product, and founders — who need data-driven answers regularly but don't want to depend on a data engineering team to get them.
